Animal Welfare Statement

At WorldFamousPets, we are deeply committed to the welfare of the exotic animals that we care for and sell. Our priority is to ensure that each animal is treated with the utmost respect and care, and we adhere to the highest standards of animal welfare throughout every step of the process. We follow strict ethical guidelines to ensure that all animals are sourced responsibly, legally, and sustainably, with a focus on conservation efforts. Our animals are housed in environments that are designed to meet their unique needs, providing them with the appropriate space, enrichment, and care. Our Key Welfare Principles Responsible Sourcing We work with licensed breeders, reputable wildlife experts, and conservation organizations to ensure that our animals come from ethically responsible sources. Proper Housing Each animal is provided with their own open-range habitat simulation shelter which will closely mimic its natural environment, promoting comfort, health, and well-being. Health and Veterinary Care All animals are regularly monitored by certified veterinarians. They receive necessary vaccinations, check-ups, and treatments to ensure optimal health. Diet and Nutrition Each animal is given a specialized diet that is carefully planned and tailored to its specific needs, ensuring they receive the proper nutrients to thrive. These meal plans are then forwarded to the eventual owner of the animal and rigorous assessment and animal welfare checks are carried out before applications for ownership are approved. If you are looking to purchase an exotic animal, our excellent team will walk you through the process ensuring you are able to get your application approved. Customer Education We provide comprehensive care guides and support to new owners, including 3 to 10 day immersive courses with assessments included at no additional cost to the prospective owner, ensuring they understand the responsibilities of caring for exotic animals, including proper habitat setup, feeding, and enrichment. Conservation and Ethics We are committed to supporting conservation efforts and raising awareness about the importance of preserving biodiversity. We only sell animals that have been bred responsibly and are not taken from the wild unless permitted under conservation efforts.

We believe that with the right care, exotic animals can thrive in captivity and make excellent, loving companions. By purchasing from us, you are supporting responsible breeding practices, animal welfare, and conservation initiatives. If you have any questions about the care of your new pet or need assistance, our team is always here to help.
